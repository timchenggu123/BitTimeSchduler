Urgency: 0-5 -used to calculate time_lag score and IU scale score. 5 should be of the utmost priority, 0 is lowest priority
Importance: 0-5 -used to calculate IU scale score. 5 should be of the utmost importance, and 0 is lowst importance.
Reschedulability: [-2,-1,0,1]. -2 is unique to free event. -1 is means that the event can be dropped without penalty. 0 means the event should not be rescheduled (for example, it would not make sense to schedule one day's breakfast to another day). 1 means the event can be scheduled.
